What is Observer Pattern?

Observer Pattern is used when one object (Subject) needs to automatically notify multiple other objects (Observers) whenever its state changes.

👉 “One changes → many get notified”

🧠 Real-World Example: YouTube Channel & Subscribers
Entities:

YouTube Channel → Subject

Subscribers → Observers

Scenario:

A YouTube channel uploads a new video

All subscribers get notified automatically

🔄 How it maps to Observer Pattern
Real World	Observer Pattern
YouTube Channel	Subject
Subscriber	Observer
Subscribe	Register observer
Unsubscribe	Remove observer
New video uploaded	State change
Notification sent	notifyObservers()




🔍 Simple Flow

Subscribers subscribe to the channel

Channel uploads a video

Channel notifies all subscribers

Subscribers receive the update
